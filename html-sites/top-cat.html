Top 2 - Cat

Cat is the second most common pet in the world. They may rival the dogs According to people. Turns out Cat's and Dog do get along together in the same household. Both have good chemistry and play together in the field.

Cat's do have its known uniqueness, they are flexible, jump higher, and sometimes they defy physics when recovering from tall heights. Cat's have multiple personality, though they may appear bored around people. With good care they can be as good as dog. They require less food and spends more time sleeping. And they are much easier to take care.

Qualities of Cats - According to pet owners

  • Loving and Caring
  • Curious
  • Playful
  • Requires less food
  • Funny
