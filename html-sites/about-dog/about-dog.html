Dog as Pets

Dog are very adorable and supportive pets, they are highly affectionate to their owner. And they act like a part of your family as it should be. They can be trained to do tricks, They can be guard of your house, and They can be playful too.

Pet Owners perspective about dogs

As a dog owner, We feel safe everytime we sleep with dog guarding the house. They are a good companion for your family. Though when it comes to taking care of dogs. They can be difficult to raise since they are large and requires a lot of calories. They are surprisingly easy to bath but you have to do it more frequently than other pets, since they barely sanitize themselves. Despite that having them with you is heartwarming since they are very loyal to you.

Endnote

Dog can live for more than 20 years, They are very loyal and pays you unconditional love. They may be hard to raise but the pros outweighs the cons. Dogs can be the best buddy of your life.
